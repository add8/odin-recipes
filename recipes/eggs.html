My Delicious Shirred Eggs

These eggs are simple yet elegant thanks to dairy and a shirring technique.

The difference between over-hammered, dry eggs and fluffy, restaurant-rich ones is just a little technique. Grab some chopsticks!

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • milk or sour cream
  • butter, oil, or margarine

Steps

  1. Grease a pan set to medium-low heat with butter, oil, or margarine.
  2. Scramble egg yolks and add milk or sour cream, mixing further.
  3. Add egg mixture to pan, continuously stirring and breaking up egg curds with chopsticks until eggs are fluffy and still slightly moist.
