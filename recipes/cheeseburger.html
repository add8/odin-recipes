My Famous Impossible Cheeseburger

This cheeseburger recipe is quick and easy for a great dinner after work.

I use an Impossible patty since I avoid red meat, but another benefit is the even cook you'll get.

Ingredients

  • Impossible burger patty
  • bun (tip: check the clearance rack at the grocery store, and freeze the whole bag)
  • American cheese
  • vegetable oil
  • hot sauce
  • mayo
  • ketchup
  • fancy mustard

Steps

  1. Heat a few teaspoons of oil in a pan set to medium high.
  2. Place patty on pan when hot.
  3. Toast bun
  4. After four minutes or when burger is brown on the bottom, flip the patty and add a slice of American cheese.
  5. Cook for four more minutes.
  6. Remove patty from heat and place on bun.
  7. Add other condiments as desired.
