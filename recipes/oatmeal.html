Oatmeal

My Hearty Oatmeal

Nothing starts the day better than hearty, helthy oatmeal that gives you the boost you need.

With this flexible recipe, the more you add to craft the perfect bowl, the better!

Ingredients

  • Steel-cut oatmeal (instant or minute-cook brands are fine, but I lean toward a slightly slower cook anyway)
  • Salt
  • flax and chia seeds (whole or ground)
  • berries (fresh or frozen)
  • walnuts (crushed or whole)
  • sweetener (maple syrup, honey, or brown sugar)
  • milk
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. Boil a small pot of water, with a dash of salt.
  2. Add oats and drop heat to let simmer.
  3. Add seeds and stir to avoid clumping.
  4. Add walnuts.
  5. After five minutes or at desired consistency, remove from heat.
  6. Add sweetener, milk, and cinnamon to taste.
